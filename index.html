GLORY STREAM CONVOCATION

The Mandate

Glory Stream Convocation is a conventional worship gathering where the true Gospel of Jesus Christ is preached, Spirit-filled praise and worship music is unleashed, and believers are immersed in the tangible presence of God to become agents of His kingdom in their world.

We are a movement and a sacred assembly. Born from a deep hunger for authentic worship and biblical truth, Glory Stream Convocation exists to bridge the gap between the reverence of the historic faith and the freedom of Spirit-led revival. We are a gathered people dedicated to experiencing the manifest presence of God and the continuous filling of the Holy Spirit.

Under the leadership of PHILPEACE, this movement is issuing a call to the nations: Come out of spiritual complacency, worship God truly and Live transformed.

The Name

GLORY

The manifest, tangible presence of God. It is the revelation of His character, His holiness, and His saving power, demonstrated fully through the life, death, and resurrection of Jesus Christ.

STREAM

The continuous, ongoing work and filling of the Holy Spirit in the life of the believer. It represents the constant supply of God’s grace, power, and life that sustains us daily.

CONVOCATION

A formal, sovereign calling together of God's people. We do not gather by accident; we are divinely summoned by the Spirit to assemble for worship, the preaching of the Word, and mutual edification.

Our Vision

The Future We See

To foster a transformational worship lifestyle, becoming a generation that carries the presence and power of God into every sphere of life.

The vision goes beyond the event. We do not merely want people to experience God's presence for a few hours in a sanctuary; we want that encounter to reshape their everyday lives. A transformational worship lifestyle means that worship is no longer just an activity, but our core identity. We envision believers who live in constant awareness of God, impacting their homes, workplaces, and communities with the reality of who He is.

{Colossians 3:17, 23, Isaiah 43:7,21}

Our Mission

To cultivate an holistic culture of worship that begins in private devotion and overflows into corporate celebration, convening Spirit-filled gatherings where the true Gospel is preached and the Holy Spirit brings life.

Our Four Pillars

01 The True Gospel – Our Anchor

Jesus Christ is the foundation of our convocation. We unashamedly preach Christ incarnate, lived a perfect/righteous life, Him crucified, risen, and returning. Every song, every prayer, and every sermon points back to the cross. We believe that experiencing God's presence is impossible apart from the saving grace of Jesus Christ.

02 Spirit-Filled Worship - Our Response

We are committed to creating an atmosphere where the Holy Spirit directs our gatherings. Our worship is not a musical performance; it is our response to God's revelation. We prioritize authentic, heartfelt praise and intimate worship, making room for the spontaneous leading of the Spirit, prophetic ministry, and spiritual gifts, all exercised biblically and in order.

03 Biblical Conviction - The Foundation

As a conventional gathering, we are deeply rooted in the authority of Scripture. We do not chase emotional experiences; we submit our emotions and experiences to the Word of God. Every manifestation is tested against Scripture, and every teaching is expository and Christ-centered.

04 Lifestyle Transformation - The Outcome

The convocation is not an endpoint; it is an equipping station. We gather to be filled, but we scatter to serve. A true encounter with the Holy Spirit always results in transformed character, bold witness, and active compassion. We are called to be salt and light, carrying the reality of our worship into our everyday environments.

The Core Message: The Gospel

The Gospel is the source of our life. Through the sacrifice of Jesus Christ on the cross, sin is forgiven and the barrier between God and man is removed. Because of the cross, the Holy Spirit is given to the believer.

We preach that salvation comes only through faith in Christ, and that the indwelling of the Holy Spirit empowers the believer to live a holy, transformed, and fruitful life. We call the church to complete surrender—yielding our will to God so that His Spirit may fully direct us.

Romans 12:1, Galatians 2:20
